04

Questions, answered

When can I get it?+

Quell is entering closed beta on Google Play. Request an invite by email and you'll get the opt-in link as soon as the beta opens.

How much will it cost?+

One single purchase at launch — no subscription, no ads, no "premium" upsell. During the beta, Quell is free for testers.

Which languages does it understand?+

Quell ships with English and Portuguese, and its triage understands notifications in both.

Why Android only?+

Only Android gives apps a supported way to manage notifications (the notification listener API). iOS doesn't — so a genuine Quell for iPhone isn't technically possible today.

What does Quell read, exactly?+

The content of incoming notifications — that's what sorting requires. It is processed on-device, kept at most 30 days in local history, and never transmitted. The full details are in the privacy policy.
